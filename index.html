About

Banglar Bondhu is a medical Robot that basically works as a nurse or an assistant of Doctors and gives company to the patients when they are having a hard time being isolated. Though these are the primary purpose of our robot, other features are also interesting. We divided it’s features mainly in 5 categories. Let's have a look!

Clinical observation and comments

Sample collection and store

Sanitization (itself and patient)

Emergency alarm and contact

Assistance and companionship
